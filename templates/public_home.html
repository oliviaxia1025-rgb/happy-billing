What we do 服务内容

Everything between the visit
and the payment. 从看诊到回款，全程代办。

Check Eligibility 查新保险

Enter a patient's DOB, insurance ID and plan — we verify coverage, acupuncture benefits, copay and remaining deductible, with results back within 24 hours. 输入病人生日、保险号与计划，我们核查针灸覆盖、自付与剩余自付额，24小时内回复。

Claims History & Appeals 申报记录 / 追款

Track every claim's status and payment. When there's a problem, we call the insurer and handle the paperwork until you're paid. 追踪每笔申报的状态与回款；遇到问题，由我们与保险公司沟通到底，直到拿到钱。
