Our story 我们的故事

Built from listening to doctors.源于对医师们的倾听。

Many of the finest acupuncturists in our community trained in China and brought decades of skill to the United States. But once here, a different barrier appeared — not in medicine, but in paperwork and English-language insurance systems.

我们社区里许多优秀的针灸医师，都在中国接受过多年训练，带着精湛医术来到美国。然而到了这里，他们面对的不是医术上的难题，而是语言与保险系统带来的障碍。

We started Happy Billing to close that gap — a dependable, Mandarin-speaking partner that verifies coverage, files clean CMS-1500 claims, and chases every payment so our doctors are never short-changed for the care they give.

于是我们创办了 Happy Billing —— 做一个值得信赖、能用中文沟通的合作伙伴：核查保险、规范申报、追踪每一笔回款，让医师们的辛劳都能得到应有的回报。

The problem we solve 我们要解决的问题

Great medicine, lost in paperwork. 好医术，不该被保险文件耽误。

Language barriers 语言障碍

Calling insurers in English, decoding EOBs and denial codes — exhausting and error-prone. 用英文打电话、看懂保险条款和拒付代码，既费力又容易出错。

Money left on the table 该拿的钱拿不到

Unfiled or rejected CMS-1500 claims mean treatment goes unpaid. 没报或被拒的申报，等于白白看了诊，拿不到保险公司的钱。

No time, no system 没时间、没系统

Tracking deductibles and follow-ups by hand steals hours from patients. 手工追踪自付额和回款，挤占了本该看诊的时间。
